## Key Teachings

### Natural Healing Philosophy
Barbara O'Neill believes in giving the body optimum conditions to heal itself. The body has innate intelligence and healing capacity when provided the right inputs.

### Minerals & Bone Health
The body requires 12 essential minerals for optimal function:
- **Sodium**: Balances fluids, nerve impulses, muscle contraction
- **Zinc**: Blood clotting, protein synthesis, immune function
- **Calcium, Magnesium, Phosphorus**: Bone density and strength
- **Copper, Iron, Manganese**: Enzyme function and oxygen transport
- **Iodine**: Thyroid function and metabolism
- **Selenium**: Antioxidant protection
- **Chromium**: Blood sugar regulation
- **Molybdenum**: Detoxification support

**Key Insight**: Mineral deficiencies are at the root of most chronic conditions. Celtic salt provides trace minerals that table salt lacks.

### Lymphatic System

- Detoxifies the body

- **Activation**: Movement, deep breathing, hydration, contrast showers
