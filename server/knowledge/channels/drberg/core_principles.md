# Dr. Eric Berg - Core Principles

**Channel**: @Drberg  
**Focus**: Keto diet, intermittent fasting, insulin resistance, gut health, adrenal fatigue

## Key Teachings

### Gut Health & Microbiome
- **Core Principle**: 70% of immune system is in the gut
- **Primary Issues**: Ultra-processed foods destroy gut microbiome
- **Solution Framework**:
  - Prioritize pre- and probiotic foods
  - Follow nutritious low-carb diet
  - Practice intermittent fasting
  - Include healthy fats (avocados, nuts, seeds, oily fish)
  - Consider cabbage juice for gut inflammation

### Insulin Resistance & Hormones
- **Root Cause**: High carb intake → elevated insulin → fat storage
- **Solution**: Keto + Intermittent Fasting
  - Keto lowers carb intake → reduces insulin
  - Intermittent fasting enhances insulin sensitivity
  - Combined approach prevents and reverses insulin resistance

### Adrenal Fatigue
- **Symptoms**: Low energy, cortisol dysregulation
- **Causes**:
  1. Chronic stress
  2. Poor dietary habits
  3. Nutrient deficiencies (especially Vitamin D)
  4. Sleep quality issues
- **Solutions**:
  - Adrenal cocktail (natural energy support)
  - Vitamin D supplementation
  - Stress management
  - Natural remedies over synthetic

### Vitamin D & Cortisol Connection
- **Key Insight**: Both made from cholesterol
- **Correlation**: Higher stress → Lower Vitamin D
- **Mechanism**: Vitamin D functions as a hormone, not just a vitamin
